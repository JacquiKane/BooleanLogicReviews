See if you can solve the following Boolean logic equations. Click the question button to check your solution. You may need to use De Morgan's Laws for some of the questions.

The 3 basic Boolean operators are AND, OR, NOT. In Java, && represents logical AND, || represents logical OR and the ! represents the negate, NOT.
NOT(a AND b) <=> NOT(a) OR NOT(b)
NOT(a OR b) <=> NOT(a) AND NOT(b)
